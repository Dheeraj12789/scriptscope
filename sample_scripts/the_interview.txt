Title: The Interview

Scene
A stark white room. A single table. Maya sits across from an interviewer in a dark suit. There's a glass of water she hasn't touched.

Dialogue
Interviewer: Tell me about your greatest failure.
Maya: I saved everyone in that building.
Interviewer: That's not what I asked.
Maya: Forty-seven people walked out alive because of me.
Interviewer: And one didn't. Tell me about that one.
Maya: (voice cracks) She was seven.
Interviewer: Her name?
Maya: You know her name. It's in your folder.
Interviewer: I want to hear you say it.
Maya: (whispers) Lily. Her name was Lily.
Interviewer: Why couldn't you save Lily, Maya?
Maya: Because I chose to save her mother instead.

[Long silence. The interviewer closes the folder. Maya finally reaches for the water.]

Interviewer: That's not a failure, Maya. That's an impossible choice.
Maya: Then why does it feel like one?
