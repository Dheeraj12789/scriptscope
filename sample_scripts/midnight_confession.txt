Title: Midnight Confession

Scene
A dimly lit parking garage. Two detectives stand beside an unmarked car. Detective Nora holds a phone showing a recording. Detective Kai hasn't moved in thirty seconds.

Dialogue
Nora: I found this on his phone. Listen.
Kai: I don't need to listen. I already know what's on it.
Nora: You... what?
Kai: I've known for three months, Nora. I've been protecting him.
Nora: Protecting a suspect? Do you understand what you're saying?
Kai: He's not a suspect. He's my brother.
Nora: (steps back) Your brother died six years ago. That's in your file.
Kai: My file says a lot of things. Not all of them are true.
Nora: If Internal Affairs finds out—
Kai: They won't. Unless you play that recording.

[Nora looks at the phone. Then at Kai. She slowly puts the phone in her pocket.]

Kai: Thank you.
Nora: Don't thank me. I haven't decided yet.
