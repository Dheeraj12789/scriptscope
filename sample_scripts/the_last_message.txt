Title: The Last Message

Scene
Riya receives a message from her ex-boyfriend after five years. She stares at her phone, hands trembling. The coffee shop around her fades into silence.

Dialogue
Riya: Why now?
Arjun: Because today I learned the truth.
Riya: What truth?
Arjun: That the accident wasn't your fault.
Riya: (long pause) You let me carry that for five years.
Arjun: I know. And I'm sorry.
Riya: Sorry doesn't give me back the years I spent hating myself.
Arjun: Then let me give you the next ones.

[Riya looks at the message. Types. Deletes. Types again. The cursor blinks.]
